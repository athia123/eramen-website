Our Story

ERamen Shop holds a distinguished position as one of the oldest ramen establishments globally, with its roots tracing back to its establishment in Japan in 1910. Since 2015, ERamen has brought its rich legacy to Jakarta, Indonesia, delighting patrons with its exceptional culinary offerings. The heart and soul of ERamen lie in its dedication to creating flavorful ramen using organic flour as the foundation for their noodles.

The meticulous craftsmanship and commitment to tradition shine through in every bowl of ramen served at ERamen. The organic flour-based noodles, carefully prepared using time-honored techniques, form the centerpiece of their menu. By infusing Indonesian flavors with the essence of Japanese cuisine, ERamen presents a truly unique dining experience that captivates ramen enthusiasts and leaves a lasting impression.

Within the walls of ERamen, patrons immerse themselves in a culinary time capsule, steeped in over a century of ramen-making heritage. The fusion of innovation and tradition is evident in every aspect, from the meticulous selection of organic ingredients sourced from local farmers to the creative twists applied to classic ramen recipes. ERamen invites guests to embark on a gastronomic adventure that celebrates the art of ramen-making, Indonesian heritage, and a relentless pursuit of culinary excellence.

Vision

To be the beloved ramen shop that brings joy and satisfaction to every customer, celebrating the art of ramen-making while surpassing expectation.

Mission

To serve exceptional ramen with the finest ingredients, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere, and delivering an oustanding dining experience that leaves lasting memories.
